I need some ideas for a clean, minimalist dashboard UI.

Here are a few concepts for a minimalist dashboard:

  • Liquid Glass: Use heavy blur (backdrop-filter), semi-transparent white cards, and subtle borders. Keep background colorful but diffused.
  • Monochrome Cards: Use a very light grey background (#F8FAFC) with pure white cards. Use deep blue or black only for text and primary actions.
  • Negative Space: Focus on typography and whitespace. Remove all dividers and rely on spacing to separate content.
